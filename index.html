About

The site is meant to provide data visualization of Singapore's population structure in a format that can be comprehended and analysed easily.

All data used can be found at the following goverment bodies' website below.

Photo by Annie Spratt on Unsplash

Department of Statistics Singapore

DOS is the source for all population structure and geographical distribution data

Urban Redevelopment Authority

URA is the source for regional boundaries, and Master Plan 2019 is used for the site
